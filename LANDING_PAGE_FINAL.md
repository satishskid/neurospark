- **Secondary:** Blue (#3b82f6) - Trust, intelligence
- **Accent:** Purple (#a855f7) - Creativity, advanced tech
- **Background:** Slate-900 (#0f172a) - Focus, sophistication
- **Text:** White/Slate-200/Slate-300 - Hierarchy, readability

### Animations
1. **Blob Animation** - Organic background movement
   - 7-second loop
   - Staggered delays (0s, 2s, 4s)
   - Subtle, not distracting

   - 3-second gentle rise/fall
   - Staggered delays (0s, 1s, 2s)

   - Smooth transitions
   - Encourages engagement

- **Subheadline:** lg-xl, medium weight
- **Body:** base-lg, regular weight
- **Trust indicators:** sm with emojis
- **Quote:** xs, italic, subtle
