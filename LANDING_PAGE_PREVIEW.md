## ğŸ¨ Visual Elements

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Cyan (#06b6d4) - Trust, technology, healthcare
- **Secondary:** Blue (#3b82f6) - Professionalism, reliability
- **Accent:** Purple (#a855f7) - Innovation, creativity
- **Background:** Slate-900 (#0f172a) - Modern, focused
- **Text:** White/Slate-200 - High contrast, readable

### Animations
1. **Blob Animation** - Organic, flowing background shapes
   - Creates sense of movement and innovation
   - Subtle, not distracting
   - 7-second loop with delays

2. **Float Animation** - Concept cards gently rise and fall
   - Adds life to static elements
   - 3-second loop
   - Staggered delays for visual interest

3. **Hover Effects** - Button scales up on hover
   - Provides tactile feedback
   - Encourages interaction

### Typography
- **Headline:** 4xl-6xl, extrabold, tight tracking
- **Subheadline:** lg-xl, medium weight
- **Body:** base-lg, regular weight
- **Trust indicators:** sm, with checkmarks
- **Quote:** xs, italic, subtle
