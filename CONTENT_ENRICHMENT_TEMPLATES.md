## MODULE 1: AI in Your Practice Today (Lessons 2-7)

### Lesson med-1-2: Your First AI Conversation
**Executive Summary**: Learn to interact effectively with AI through natural language. This lesson provides hands-on experience with AI systems, teaching you how to structure queries, interpret responses, and iterate for better results. Practice with non-PHI examples builds confidence before clinical use.

**Key Topics**:
- Natural language interaction basics
- Structuring effective queries
- Interpreting AI responses
- Iterative refinement techniques
- Common mistakes and how to avoid them

**Case Studies**:
1. Emergency physician using AI for differential diagnosis support
2. Primary care doctor generating patient education materials
3. Hospitalist using AI for discharge summary optimization

**Practical Exercises**:
1. Practice conversation: Ask AI to explain a complex medical concept
2. Iterative refinement: Start with vague prompt, refine to specific output
3. Error analysis: Identify what went wrong in failed AI interactions

**Key Takeaways**:
- AI responds to natural language - no coding required
- Specificity improves output quality
- Iteration is normal and expected
- Context matters for relevant responses
- Practice builds confidence and skill
