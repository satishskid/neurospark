## üéØ Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

1. **Understand AI Fundamentals**
   - Explain how AI learns and makes predictions
   - Identify appropriate use cases in healthcare
   - Understand technical concepts (tokens, context, parameters)

2. **Ensure HIPAA Compliance**
   - Identify all 18 HIPAA identifiers
   - De-identify patient data correctly
   - Choose HIPAA-compliant AI tools

3. **Write Effective Prompts**
   - Use 4-part prompt formula
   - Create prompts for clinical workflows
   - Optimize for accuracy and safety

4. **Recognize AI Risks**
   - Identify hallucinations and errors
   - Recognize bias in AI outputs
   - Implement safety measures

5. **Build AI Workflows**
   - Analyze workflows for AI opportunities
   - Design HIPAA-compliant implementations
   - Measure success and ROI

6. **Implement Ethically**
   - Apply transparency principles
   - Ensure equity across populations
   - Maintain human oversight

## üìñ Module Breakdown

### Module 1: AI in Your Practice Today (18 min)
**Key Concepts:** AI applications, 3 Rules of Safe AI Use, practical examples  
**Assessment:** 5-question scenario-based quiz  
**Teaching Tips:** Emphasize real time savings, safety-first approach

### Module 2: How AI Thinks (26 min)
**Key Concepts:** Pattern recognition, models, parameters, tokens, context  
**Medical Analogies:** Parameters = years of training, Tokens = abbreviations  
**Assessment:** 3-question technical quiz  
**Teaching Tips:** Use medical analogies throughout

### Module 3: Protecting Patient Privacy (20 min)
**Key Concepts:** HIPAA, 18 identifiers, de-identification, BAAs  
**Assessment:** Quiz + hands-on de-identification exercise  
**Teaching Tips:** Emphasize legal consequences, provide real examples

### Module 4: Advanced Prompt Engineering (22 min)
**Key Concepts:** 4-part formula, documentation, decision support  
**Assessment:** Quiz on prompt structure  
**Teaching Tips:** Show before/after examples of good prompts

### Module 5: AI Safety & Bias (19 min)
**Key Concepts:** Hallucinations, bias recognition, ethics framework  
**Assessment:** Quiz on safety and bias  
**Teaching Tips:** Use real examples of AI errors in healthcare

### Module 6: Building Workflows (24 min)
**Key Concepts:** Workflow analysis, tool selection, ROI  
**Assessment:** Quiz on workflow design  
**Teaching Tips:** Help learners identify their own use cases

### Module 7: Capstone Project (90 min)
**Components:**
- Knowledge assessment (15 questions)
- Prompt engineering exercise
- De-identification exercise
- Case study analysis
- AI action plan creation

**Grading:** Minimum 75% on knowledge assessment + completion of all exercises

## üéì Facilitation Tips

### For Live Sessions
1. Start with poll: "Who has used AI in their practice?"
2. Share real examples from your institution
3. Do live de-identification demo
4. Have learners share their AI action plans

### For Self-Paced Learning
1. Encourage use of AI tutor for questions
2. Suggest completing 1-2 modules per day
3. Recommend taking notes on action plan ideas
4. Provide forum for peer discussion

### Common Questions

**Q: "Is AI going to replace doctors?"**  
A: No. AI assists, you decide. Emphasize human judgment is irreplaceable.

**Q: "Can I use ChatGPT with patient data?"**  
A: Only if de-identified OR if using enterprise version with BAA.

**Q: "What if I make a HIPAA mistake?"**  
A: Report immediately, follow incident response protocol.
